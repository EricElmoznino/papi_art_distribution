How This Works

Before you choose

Use credits to show which paintings would mean the most to you.

This is a little like an auction, except the credits are not money and nobody is paying for anything. Everyone privately makes one set of choices up front, and those choices are used later to help assign the paintings.

You will start with 100 credits. Put more credits on paintings you would be especially happy to receive, and fewer credits on paintings that are still meaningful but less important to you. Please leave a painting at zero if you would not want to receive it.

Once you have allocated your credits, you can copy your choices to the clipboard using a special button, and then send them to me by text message.

After everyone has submitted their choices, the process uses the credits as preference signals. It tries to share paintings broadly among the people who wanted them, and exact ties are handled automatically.
